# BUILD A FULL-STACK WEB APPLICATION CALLED "AfraLink "

Create a modern, mobile-first Progressive Web App (PWA) called **AfraLink **

AfraLink is a transportation and logistics marketplace for Southern Nigeria.

The platform connects customers directly with:

* Private car drivers
* Taxi drivers
* Keke riders
* Motorcycle riders
* Bus drivers
* Mini-bus drivers
* Van drivers
* Pickup drivers
* Truck drivers
* Lorry drivers
* Delivery drivers
* Logistics operators
* Car rental owners

IMPORTANT:

AfraLink  does NOT process payments.

Customers contact service providers directly and negotiate prices themselves.

The platform acts only as a marketplace connecting customers with verified drivers and vehicle owners.

# DRIVER VERIFICATION

Driver registration requires:

## Personal Information

* Full Name
* Phone Number
* Email Address
* Gender
* Date of Birth

## Residential Information

* Full Address
* State
* City

## Vehicle Information

* Vehicle Type
* Vehicle Brand
* Vehicle Model
* Vehicle Color
* Plate Number

## Verification Documents

Mandatory:

* NIN Number
* NIN Slip Image
* Driver Selfie
* Vehicle Photo
* Driver Profile Photo

Drivers remain pending until approved by Admin.
